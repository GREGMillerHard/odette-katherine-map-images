Katherine Mansfield

    Odette Hallowes

      Katherine was born in Wellington, New Zealand, 14 October 1888. She sailed for London in 1908 and never returned. The map begins where her European life begins.

      Marker positions are town-level. Sites within towns (Villa Augusta, Higher Tregerthen, Le Prieuré, Burvale Cemetery) sit at the locality centre. The Gourock to Gibraltar line shows a standard wartime convoy course out of the Clyde. Her ship’s exact track is not documented in the project sources. Every event shown is documented in the treatment and its sources.
